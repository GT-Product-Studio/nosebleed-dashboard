How It Works

F5 Innings Under

Predicts total runs scored in the first 5 innings. Blends pitcher ERA (season + last 5 starts), opponent runs/game, and park run factors. Only fires when both starters have recent ERA under 3.80. 2-year backtest: +13.9% ROI across 1,976 plays.

Pitcher K Props (Over)

Projects strikeout totals for starting pitchers. Combines K/9 rate, recent K averages, opponent strikeout susceptibility (K/PA), and ballpark K factors. Weights season and recent performance 50/50. 2-year backtest: +6.0% ROI (1,147 plays), +10.2% at 12%+ edge.

IP Props (Outs Recorded)

Predicts whether a pitcher goes deep or gets pulled early. Under signals trigger on short recent IP averages, short rest, or high pitch counts. Over signals require 6.5+ IP average and a strikeout-prone opponent. 2-year backtest: +11.8% ROI (1,630 plays), +19.4% at 12%+ edge.
